Pramukh Swami Maharaj

Pramukh Swami Maharaj is my role model. He is my inspiration because of how he lives his life. I strive to embibe his motto "In the joy of others, lies our own" into my life. He is my spiritual guide through my life and countless other people's lives.

From the images above he strived to connect with youths. He taught them spiritual values, which helped them become a better person. By seeing these images, I understood the value of a Guru (guide), a person who can bring me from the darkness to light. That is who Pramukh Swami Maharaj is to me.

There is a special memeory attached to the last two pictures. All the youths were having a sports summer camp in the Pocconos that year. And when Pramukh Swami Maharaj came, we asked him to play a couple of sports with use. Unknown to us, he had a frozen shoulder at the time. He played with us even though he was in pain, to make us happy.

Although he passed away in 2016, his work inspired many people. This video reminds me of excatly that. It teaches me how to live a life in the joy of others.
