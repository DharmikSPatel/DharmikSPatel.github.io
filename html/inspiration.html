Pramukh Swami Maharaj

Pramukh Swami Maharaj is my role model. He is my inspiration because of how he lives his life. I strive to imbibe his motto, "In the joy of others lies our own," into my life. He is my spiritual guide through my life and countless other people's lives.

From the pictures above, he strived to connect with youths. He taught them spiritual values, which helped them become a better person. By seeing these images, I understood the importance of a Guru (guide), a person who can bring me from the darkness to light. That is who Pramukh Swami Maharaj is to me.

There is a special memory attached to the last two pictures. All the youths had a sports summer camp in the Poconos that year. And when Pramukh Swami Maharaj came, we asked him to play a couple of sports with us. Unknown to us, he had a frozen shoulder at the time. He played with us even though he was in pain to make us happy.

Although he passed away in 2016, his work inspired many people. This video reminds me of exactly that. It teaches me how to live a life in the joy of others.
